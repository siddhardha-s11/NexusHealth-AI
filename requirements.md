# NexusHealth AI - Requirements

## Problem
India’s healthcare system faces high patient load, manual documentation burden, fragmented medical data, and limited patient understanding, especially in rural and Tier-2/Tier-3 regions.

## Objective
Build an AI-powered healthcare assistant that improves clinical efficiency and patient care across Bharat.

## Users
- Doctors and Hospitals
- Patients
- Healthcare Administrators

## Key Features
- Patient history summarization
- Voice-to-clinical notes
- Smart triage and risk alerts
- Multilingual patient support
- Automated reminders

## Impact
- Reduce doctor workload by 40–60%
- Improve healthcare access in rural areas
